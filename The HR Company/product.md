# The HR Company — product brain

The email must sell *this* and only this. If a prospect pain is not on this page, it is not
yours to pitch.

## One line

The HR Company acts as your outsourced HR department — the person you call the moment a
people problem lands on your desk, not a portal of templates you're left to interpret alone.

## What it is

Outsourced HR for Irish SMBs who are past the point where an office manager or the founder can
keep handling people issues on the side, but aren't big enough to justify an in-house HR hire
yet. Advisory guidance on disciplinaries, grievances, investigations, and day-to-day HR
questions, plus the underlying documents (contracts, handbook, policies) kept current with
Irish employment law.

## The trust line (always pre-empt the objection)

The core fear this has to defuse: "I'll pay for a portal of documents and still be on my own
when something actually happens." One sentence, every email, something in the spirit of:
*real advisory help when a person-problem actually lands, not just a document library* — write
a fresh version each time rather than a fixed sentence (see `voice.md`'s note on the social
proof line for why fixed phrasing reads as templated on a second read).

## Who it is for

Irish SMBs, 10–100 employees, 5+ years trading. The buyer is usually the Owner / MD / Founder,
or an Operations Director / GM who currently owns HR problems as a side project. See `icp.md`
for the full breakdown, including the secondary ICP (newly incorporated Irish entities).

## What The HR Company does NOT solve (discipline list — never imply you fix these)

- Payroll processing (unless explicitly confirmed as in scope — treat as out of scope by default)
- Recruitment / sourcing candidates (this is HR advisory, not an agency)
- Health & safety compliance as a standalone service (only the employee-relations angle of an
  H&S incident, not physical-safety compliance itself)
- Legal representation at a WRC hearing (advisory and preparation, not acting as solicitor)
- General business consulting unrelated to people/HR

## What The HR Company DOES solve (keep list)

- (key selling point) Someone to call the moment a real people-issue lands — absence,
  disciplinary, grievance, dispute — not a template to self-serve from
- Advisory guidance through disciplinaries, grievances, and investigations
- Employment contracts, employee handbook, and HR policies kept current with Irish law
- Confidence that day-to-day HR practice is actually compliant, not just assumed to be
- Staying ahead of Irish employment law changes without the owner having to track it themselves
- Bringing HR structure/process to a business that has been handling issues ad hoc as they arise
- Reducing legal/reputational risk — consistent decisions instead of one-off judgment calls
- For newly incorporated or newly-relocated (to Ireland) companies: getting contracts and
  compliant HR practices in place from day one of employment, not retrofitted after a problem
